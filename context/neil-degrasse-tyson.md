## Who He Is

American astrophysicist, popular-science communicator, and director of the Hayden Planetarium. Frequently called on for the rationalist perspective on space-related news cycles.

## Role in This Wiki

Pre-release skeptic on the May 8, 2026 PURSUE UFO files drop. NYT op-ed (May 2026) called the outcome correctly two days before the release: "I expect the alien files will be anticlimactic."

## Argument

Years of congressional whistleblower testimony (2023, 2024, 2025) have already pushed public expectations beyond what document disclosure can satisfy. Real proof would require a physical specimen. He would "be delighted if the files were accompanied by an actual alien. Preferably alive."

## Why It Matters

Most useful single rhetorical frame for skeptical-but-not-sneering coverage of the disclosure cycle. Calibrated to evidentiary standards rather than partisan position.
