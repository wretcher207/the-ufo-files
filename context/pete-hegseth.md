## Who He Is

US Secretary of War (rebranded role formerly known as Secretary of Defense) under the Trump administration. Public face of the May 8, 2026 PURSUE UFO files release.

## Role in the UFO Disclosure

Spokesperson and signatory for the PURSUE tranche. Most-quoted official across coverage. Appears at the top of every major article on the release alongside DNI Tulsi Gabbard, FBI Director Kash Patel, and NASA Administrator Jared Isaacman.

## Quotes (Verbatim)

- "These files, hidden behind classifications, have long fueled justified speculation, and it's time the American people see it for themselves."
- The Trump administration's "earnest commitment to unprecedented transparency."

## Framing Strategy

Casts prior administrations as having "sought to discredit or dissuade the American people." Positions PURSUE as fulfilling public demand for transparency rather than reacting to political pressure.
