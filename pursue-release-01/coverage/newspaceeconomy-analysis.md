## Summary

Most useful single source for understanding what the release actually proves and does not prove. Frames the limitations honestly: "unresolved" means insufficient data, not evidence of anything extraordinary. Maps the gap between what the files show and what would actually constitute proof.

## Key Points

### What "Unresolved" Actually Means
"Unresolved means the government has not made a definitive determination about the observed phenomenon." Lack of data, not presence of anomaly. This single sentence reframes most of the breathless coverage.

### Evidentiary Gaps in the Material Itself
- Sensor imagery "may lack scale, range, or full motion context."
- Without metadata, platform details, and environmental information, "viewers can overread shapes, speed, size, or apparent motion."
- The infrared specks moving across the screen pattern is exactly this overreading risk.

### Comparison to Prior Authoritative Reviews
- **AARO 2024 historical record**: "no evidence of extraterrestrial origin for the historical UFO and UAP cases reviewed."
- **NASA 2023 study**: UAP investigation is "a data problem" requiring better collection methods, not proof of alien life.

### Release Composition
- 162 files, 17 highlighted public image assets in the official carousel.
- Mix of infrared stills, Department of War case imagery, one composite sketch, archival NASA Apollo 17 material.
