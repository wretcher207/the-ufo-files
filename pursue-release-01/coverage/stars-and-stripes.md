## Summary

Military-press treatment of the release. Names the three regional combatant commands that supplied "unresolved" videos and gives the cleanest description of the September 2023 bronze ellipsoid case, which is genuinely strange.

## Key Points

### The Three Commands That Submitted Unresolved Videos
- US Indo-Pacific Command (INDOPACOM).
- US Central Command (CENTCOM).
- US European Command (EUCOM).

### September 2023 — Bronze Ellipsoid
- Ellipsoid bronze metallic object allegedly materialized out of bright light.
- Corroborating eyewitness reports.
- FBI lab-rendered graphics overlay accompanies the file.
- Most evidentially layered case in the release.

### Apollo 12 + Indo-Pacific
- Apollo 12 (1969) photographs include unidentified phenomena visible in the images.
- 2024 INDOPACOM football-shaped UAP near Japan.

### PURSUE Architecture
- Full name: Presidential Unsealing and Reporting System for UAP Encounters.
- Agencies: AARO, White House, ODNI, Department of Energy, NASA, FBI.
- Portal: war.gov/ufo.

### Officials
- Hegseth, Gabbard, Isaacman quoted as elsewhere.
- Gabbard's added framing: "the beginning of a continuing process" with "careful, comprehensive and unprecedented review."
