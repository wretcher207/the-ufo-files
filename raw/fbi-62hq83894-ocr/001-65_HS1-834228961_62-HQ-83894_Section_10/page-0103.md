UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE
FEDERAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION
Dallas, Texas
October 10, 1947

RE: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS;
INFORMATION CONCERNING
DALLAS, TEXAS
OCTOBER 9, 1947

A young white female who refused to give her name appeared at the Dallas FBI office on October 9, 1947. She stated she is interested in Unidentified Flying Objects (UFO) and has received a quantity of information concerning beings from outer space. She stated she will not reveal her identity and feels like it is not [illegible] if this information is true. However, she will meet with interested officials and furnish all the information she has provided, nothing is done to endanger her safety.

She stated in July, 1947, she met a being from [redacted]. He gave her certain [redacted] information. She was then picked up from the [redacted] and then she stated August 22, 1947, she refused to discuss [redacted]. She stated these sources told her of the following:

(1) An unidentified missile was fired at a UO over Africa on May 22, 1947, but the UO protected it by "force field".

(2) A UO was detected 2,000 miles from Earth by [redacted] August 9, 1947.

(3) A UO was detected over Antarctica August 20, 1947.

(4) A UO was detected over the "Dewline" in [redacted] and was shot down, and beings from outer space are trying to recover it.

If she will know other information, [illegible] information would furnish on further details.
