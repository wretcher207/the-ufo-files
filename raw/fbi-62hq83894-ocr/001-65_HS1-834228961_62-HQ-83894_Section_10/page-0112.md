# 65_HS1-834228961_62-HQ-83894_Section_10 - Page 112

UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT
Memorandum

TO: DIRECTOR, FBI
FROM: SAC, DALLAS (89-1371) (C)
SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS - INFORMATION CONCERNING

There is enclosed for the Bureau one Xerox copy of a letter dated 11/20/67 from MR. ROBERT C. EDWARDS, NORTHHAVEN, Dallas, Texas, received by the Dallas office on 11/21/67, as enclosed in an envelope marked "Unidentified Flying Objects - Information Concerning".

The enclosed letter reflects the information of a phenomenon (NICAP) in the Dallas area - the National Investigations Committee on Aerial Phenomena which is organization to conduct scientific investigation of unidentified flying objects.

This letter has been acknowledged and a copy of same has been furnished to Tinker AFB for its information.

The acknowledgement to MR. EDWARDS pointed out to him that information concerning unidentified flying objects received by the FBI is, by requirement, furnished to the U. S. Air Force.
