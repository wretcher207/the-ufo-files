INFORMATION CONCERNING UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECT AT MILWAUKEE, WISCONSIN

AT 12:15 P.M., 8/22/74, [redacted] (OCD) Milwaukee Division received a telephone call from Major [redacted] (National Guard). Major [redacted] asked for information concerning an object which fell from the sky at Milwaukee, Wisconsin. He had been informed by local police and turned over to the Milwaukee office of the FBI.

No information available at Milwaukee Division. Major [redacted] advised that an unidentified object had been reported by Milwaukee Police at 8/22/74. He stated that it was a 3 inch object in metallic substance and color. The Milwaukee FBI office was asked to report on the object which was known about the object and [redacted] had an "actual source".

Above information was relayed to Major [redacted] with request to contact Milwaukee Police Department.
