Office Memorandum • UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

DATE: 8-25-52

TO: DIRECTOR, FBI

FROM: SAC, PORTLAND

SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED OBJECTS

The following investigation was conducted at Green River, Utah.

On August 21, 1952, [redacted] reported that at approximately 10:00 A.M. he observed an object east of Green River, Utah. The object was moving in a northerly direction and was approximately 8,000 feet in the air. The object was described as being metallic and appeared to be circular in shape. It was estimated that the object was moving at a speed of approximately 400 miles per hour. The object was observed to be hovering and then moving at a high rate of speed. It was observed to be moving in a circular pattern and then it would dart off in a straight line. The object was observed to be approximately 30 feet in diameter. It was estimated that the object was at an altitude of 30 feet above the ground. The object was observed to be moving in the same position as near [redacted] and then it darted off in the same manner as the object described above.

It is noted that [redacted] serves as a [redacted] in the U.S. Navy. [redacted] is currently on leave for [redacted] days and is reported to be residing in [redacted].

The above information was received by [redacted] of the [redacted] office. [redacted] stated that he had been observing the object for approximately 10 minutes. He stated that the object appeared to be metallic and was approximately 30 feet in diameter. He stated that the object was moving at a speed of approximately 400 miles per hour and that it was moving in a circular pattern.
