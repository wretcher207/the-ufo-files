UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

DATE: Sept 28, 1947
TO: SAC, San Francisco
FROM: DIRECTOR, FBI
SUBJECT: FLYING DISCS

Enclosed is a copy of a letter dated August 28, 1947, from J. Edgar Hoover to S-A, Anchorage Field Office, with the sighting of a flying object by [redacted] and his co-pilot [redacted] near Alaska.

Photostatic copies of this letter and attachment are being forwarded to the Anchorage Field Office. It is requested that you interview [redacted] and his co-pilot [redacted] in order to obtain all details of the reported sighting of a flying object on August 28, 1947. The San Francisco Field Office should be furnished copies of the results of these interviews for distribution to the Sixth Army.
