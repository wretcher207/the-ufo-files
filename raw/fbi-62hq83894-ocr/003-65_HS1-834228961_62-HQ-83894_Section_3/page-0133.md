Boys coming ACROSS River from N side of Boat.

A close up view of the Flying saucer as it
passed by the Auger Falls Trout Farm
at 1:00 pm Aug 13 going down Snake River
Canyon. at a height of about 75 ft from
canyon floor at a speed of about 1000 per.
I had a side view at a distance of about
300 ft. and almost on a level, and two
of my boys was below me, and saw it
at about a 45 degree angle they got both
a bottom and side view. we were all looking
from the south side looking toward the
north. the boys saw it coming about a 1/2 mi up canyon
we all lost sight of it in less than a mile.
It was all one color sort of a light sky blue
with a red tublar fiery glow on the side of hood.
The canyon floor is rough along here and it
rode up an down over the hills and hollows
It is my opinion that it is guided by instruments
and must be powered by Atomic power
as it made very little noise just a swish as
as it passed by
