Flying Discs

Dear Sir,

They say that if we are crazy, we are the last to know it. That may be my case, but I hope not. You know about 2 months ago, a number of people said they saw flying discs and the papers said it was imagination. I cannot believe hundreds of people could imagine the same thing, at the same time especially, when separated by thousands of miles.
