# 65_HS1-834228961_62-HQ-83894_Section_4 - Page 15

Mrs Milo A. Durand
Elizabethtown
New York

Dear Sir,

With all these forest fires in main and out west in our Country I was wondering whether the flying discs had anything to do with it. Was that just a myth or did they exist. If so could not they have been incendiary falling to earth then with drought and heat from the sun ignite. Were not the discs seen flying over Main Vt. and western states are the western states that have forest fires the same ones reported to have seen the flying discs.

I have great respect for your bureau or should I say our. In every way the F.B.I. has been excellent. I visited the Bureau while in Washington some years ago.

I do hope I haven't taken up to much time but couldn't stop thinking of it. All Americans must be alert but not over
We have quite a lot of different foreigners settling up here with plenty of money. Like the back woods I guess. Keep to themselves too.
