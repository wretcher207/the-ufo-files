# 65_HS1-834228961_62-HQ-83894_Section_4 - Page 29

Covington Ohio.
Jan 29, 1948.

F. B. I.
Washington D.C.
Dear Sirs:

I have done a lot of thinking about those flying discs ever since your operators called at our home. I have figured out a possible solution that I am passing on to you. If you can see the picture as I see it.

If self propelled bombs were made to travel [illegible] a few years ago, there isn't any reason to believe they could not be made to go 3000 miles today. If a foreign country were planning to drop a lot of such bombs on our cities, they would want the exact range, and the only way they could get it would be to make some tests. I
