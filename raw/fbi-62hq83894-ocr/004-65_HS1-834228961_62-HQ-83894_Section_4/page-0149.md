[Handwritten note, top right]
Flying Saucers

Hon. J. Edgar Hoover:
Washington, D.C.

Having read the enclosed clipping you will find with this letter prompts me to send it to you and ask you to check the National Inventors Council files on the 2 types of engines & the flying saucer design that I submitted to Mrs. Eleanor Roosevelt in 1942, while Henry Wallace was Secretary of Commerce who was in charge of the National Inventors Council.

I mean just this. See how easy it would have been for Wallace to have conveyed these designs to Russia and withheld them from our own Engineering Dept. You can make the biggest find here of your life, if you look.

I will appreciate a reply.

Bailey L. Davis,
141 Claire Dr. S.E.
Atlanta, Ga.
