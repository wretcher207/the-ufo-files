Office Memorandum - UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

TO: DIRECTOR, FBI
FROM: SAC, ALEXANDRIA
SUBJECT: MISCELLANEOUS INFORMATION CONCERNING [redacted]

For the user's information, several citizens in the Alexandria area have recently reported seeing flying saucers. The daily paper has carried numerous articles concerning this.

A recent meeting of the Young Men's Business Club in Alexandria suggested that since [redacted] is known as a convention city, the club should be promoting a convention of flying saucer [illegible] and was picked up by the Alexandria paper.

A member of the Young Men's Business Club advised that they have received a large number of requests regarding the convention of flying saucer [illegible] from parts of the United States. [redacted] advised by their inquiries that they are stating they want to attend such a convention.

This gentleman further advised that in view of the volume of inquiries it might be noted that the correspondence is coming in to the office of [redacted]. The Alexandria Field Office has been required to plan a flying saucer convention that they are interested in informing [redacted] of such convention were held.

This information is being furnished as it is possible that [redacted] may cause people to visit the Alexandria area. Such solicitations might be made so that they might attend such a convention to become official delegates [redacted].
