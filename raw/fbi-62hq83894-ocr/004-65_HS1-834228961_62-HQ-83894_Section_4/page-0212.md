# 65_HS1-834228961_62-HQ-83894_Section_4 - Page 212

Federal Bureau of Investigation
United States Department of Justice
Washington, D.C.

June 8, 1949

Director, FBI

RE: FLYING DISCS

Reference is made to letter dated September 9, 1947, concerning REPORTS OF FLYING DISCS. The attention of the Director was called to the result of an interview conducted by Special Agent [redacted] of the U.S. Air Force with WILLIAM ALBERT RHODES, Phoenix, Arizona, regarding his observation and photographing of a flying object which was considered by the Air Force of significance.

On June 9, 1949, S.A. [redacted] of the PHOENIX OFFICE was called to this office and advised that he had been requested by the Air Force to return the photographs concerning this interview since the information was needed by the Air Force.

When mentioned by his supervisor concerning the matter, S.A. [redacted] stated his desire to secure the substance of the information to the Phoenix Office and was informed that the interview as set forth in the letter of reference has now been furnished with a third memorandum for the FBI in order that the information is available to the FBI.

A memorandum setting forth this information has been prepared by this office and is attached to the file. It should be made available to the FBI.
