You will recall that Mr. Ernest Cuneo advised the Bureau of a letter which Walter Winchell had received from an individual in Los Angeles indicating that he had seen a flying saucer leave the ground within his view and in which letter he theorized that this might have been a landing from another planet. [Handwritten note: 83-1]

The Los Angeles Office was requested to locate and interview the writer of this letter, Peter Cameron Jones, 164 West 37th Street, Los Angeles 7, California.

The Los Angeles Office has now advised that the present owner and former manager at this address have no knowledge of any Peter Cameron Jones and that other investigation to locate Jones was unproductive. It would appear, therefore, that this may have been a prank.

You may desire to advise Mr. Cuneo that there is no such person at the address given inasmuch as he indicated that Mr. Winchell intended to do a column on this letter.
