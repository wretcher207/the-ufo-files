Actually I had never, while he lived with us in all those year ever heard him claim he had dreamed; so my only interpretation of the dream & what he said in it would mean that what "I dreamed" etc. When he was young he was ordered to "goose-step" into Prussia in the Franco-Prussian War, and he often told me in German how he hated war, & I know how much he loved the beautiful & how really kind he was. He loved garden, flowers & did beautiful cabinet work.

I might add now that I did not sign my name to the letters I wrote about that dream to the three Washington gentlemen, I was afraid to for we were so close to war with Germany then.
