Office Memorandum - UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

TO: DIRECTOR, FBI
FROM: SAC, WASHINGTON FIELD
DATE: June 29, 1950
SUBJECT: FLYING DISCS - INTERNAL SECURITY - X

This is to advise the Bureau that on June 29, 1950, HARRISON [redacted], 3333 [redacted] Place, N.W., Telephone [redacted], who is employed at the Hot Shoppe, Connecticut Ave., N.W., informed that at 2:50 P.M. that date, while looking out of the window of his residence, he believed the flying saucer to be described this object as [redacted]. He estimated the height of this object to be 20 to 25 feet and presenting a silver appearance. HARRISON stated the size at that distance - about four inches. He further stated the object appeared to be moving in a first [illegible] direction. He advised that this is the first flying saucer he has ever seen.

The foregoing is being furnished for the information of the Bureau and no action is being taken by this office.
