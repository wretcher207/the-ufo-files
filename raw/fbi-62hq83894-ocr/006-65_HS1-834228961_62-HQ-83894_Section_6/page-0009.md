# 65_HS1-834228961_62-HQ-83894_Section_6 - Page 9

Office Memorandum
UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

Mr. Cornelius of Philadelphia called to report that he and several others observed an object in the sky over Philadelphia. He described the object as being round and stated that he observed it through a telescope. He stated that he was the first to observe it.

The officers noticed the object and described it as being a red-ball and stated that the officers were in a field and saw it go out of sight. They noted that the object was seen for some time and attempted to photograph the object. Leaving a period of about 25 minutes which the officers spent trying to photograph it. The object was 50 feet in the air and heard the sound of the craft fall.

Mr. Cornelius said that he called the police and that the individual who he spoke with did not know anything about the object and was not interested in taking reports of it.

Mr. Cornelius desired to know what action should be taken.

I told Mr. Cornelius that he was correct in referring this matter to the office of the FBI and that I should direct him to the appropriate information concerning the object that we have on file. I would advise him to contact the [illegible]
